## Introduction
Various works have shown singing destorts tone contours in tonal language and thus hinders perception of the lyrics, especially when the singing pitch is high. This project aims to study **if the pitch change in singing (Happy Birthday) versus speaking is greater in tonal lanaguges (Mandarin) than in non-tonal languages (English).**


## Methodology
- 3 participants: 2 English speakers; 1 Mandarin Speaker
- Song choice: Happy Birthday Song (easy to sing)
- Sing the full song twice; asked to use their real voice
- Read the full lyrics twice
